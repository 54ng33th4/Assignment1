God's Own Country

With the Arabian Sea in the west & a channel of backwaters running throughout the state, Kerala indeed offers the most scenic backdrop for all your clicks! A horde of coconut trees can be witnessed, and its turquoise blue waters will surely take your breath away.

The Paradise of Ayurveda

Ayurveda, an ancient Indian healing tradition that's over 5,000 years old, combines lifestyle practices with yoga & healthy eating. Ayurveda is also used for beauty, massage & hair treatment. Many Ayurvedic spas and resorts are located in Kerala due to its warm climate and the abundant supply of medicinal plants.

Unique Culture & Traditions

Kerala's culture is mainly deriving from a greater Tamil-heritage region known as Tamilakam. Later, it was elaborated on through centuries of contact with overseas cultures. Native performing arts include Koodiyattom, Kathakali, Mohiniaattam, Thullal, Theyyam etc.

The spice coast of India

Kerala is famous for its spices. It flavours an assortment of spices originating from the cultures of Jews, Christians, Muslims and merchants who settled down in this state. Thus, you can find a creamy texture and aroma to the original cuisine of Kerala.

India’s first digital state

In February 2016, India’s then President, Pranab Mukherjee, conferred Kerala with the title of first digital state in the country. Kerala has the highest telecom literacy in the country, with almost 100% mobile connectivity and 75% internet connectivity.
